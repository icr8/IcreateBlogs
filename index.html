WILD LIFE

KEY FEATURES OF ORGANIC FARMING

Protecting soil quality using organic material and encouraging biological activity • Indirect provision of crop nutrients using soil microorganisms • Nitrogen fixation in soils using legumes • Weed and pest control based on methods like crop rotation, biological diversity, natural predators, organic manures and suitable chemical, thermal and biological intervention • Rearing of livestock, taking care of housing, nutrition, health, rearing and breeding • Care for the larger environment and conservation of natural habitats and wildlife
